\subsection{\-Detailed \-Description}
\-A class for encapsulating all data for a glu\-Perspective and a glu\-Look\-At call. \-These include\-: \-\_\-\-Fovy\-: \-Opening angle of frustum in y-\/direction (45Â° is a good average to start with) \-\_\-\-Aspect\-: \-The aspect ratio of the viewport (width / height) \-\_\-\-Near and \-\_\-\-Far clipping plane distances \-\_\-\-Center\-: \-The 3d-\/point to appear in the center of the viewport \-\_\-\-Camera\-: \-The 3d-\/point where the camera is \-\_\-\-Up\-: \-The 3d-\/vector that points upwards in the viewport

\-Use the \doxyref{apply()}{p.}{d1/d8f/classGLPerspective_a91531aa2c2fab0c53a49c0fac32aac56} function to transfer the perspective settings to the \-Open\-G\-L matrices.
